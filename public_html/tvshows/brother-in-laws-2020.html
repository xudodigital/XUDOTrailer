Aliyans (Brother-in-Laws) is a hit Malayalam sitcom on Kaumudy TV, directed by Rajesh Thalachira. It premiered on February 24, 2020, as a sequel to Aliyan vs Aliyan (Brother-in-Law vs Brother-in-Law). Set in Thiruvananthapuram, the show follows Ratnamma and her family, centering on the love-hate dynamic between brothers-in-law Kanakan and Cleatus. Most episodes revolve around Cleatus landing in trouble thanks to his quirky ideas, with Kanakan often stepping in to save the day.
